Explore NP's Popular Clubs & Societies!

The NP Japanese Cultural Club, founded in 1998, offers students the opportunity to explore Japan and its diverse culture through four divisions: Jinsei, Comink, Cosplay & J-Fashion, and Taiko.

Contact us at: cca_japanese@connect.np.edu.sg
Join the Korean Cultural Club, supported by the Singapore Korean Embassy, and become part of a vibrant community that celebrates and educates others about Korean culture.

Contact us at: cca_korean@connect.np.edu.sg
NP Malay Cultural Club consists of five vibrant departments: Satria Sukma (Guys Dikir), Adiratna (Girls Dikir), Itra Tari (Dance), Sri Nadi (Drama), and Cendekiawan (Debate).

Contact us at: cca_malay@connect.np.edu.sg
Think you can unleash an arrow to fly at 150 mph? You can, at NP Archery Club! Since 1998, the club has been a haven for passionate archers, with members excelling in national-level competitions.

Contact us at: cca_archery@connect.np.edu.sg
NP Inline Skating Club welcomes you. We focus on two exciting disciplines in competitions: freestyle slide and classic slalom. In the slide discipline, skaters execute various slides with unique leg positions.

Contact us at: cca_inline@connect.np.edu.sg
NP Water Polo Club is a fast-paced sport that requires strong tactics, teamwork, hand-eye coordination, and fitness. Join us and be part of a dynamic and competitive team!

Contact us at: cca_waterpolo@connect.np.edu.sg
At the NP Astronomy Club, our passion for the stars extends beyond campus! As interest in the sciences grows, we actively share our knowledge of astronomy with the public.

Contact us at: cca_astronomy@connect.np.edu.sg
Join NP Tabletop Games Club and dive into a world of strategy, social interaction, and endless fun. We have a collection of over 100 games.

Contact us at: cca_tabletopgames@connect.np.edu.sg
The Society of Animal Rangers (SOAR) aims to increase awareness of and encourage member involvement in the rescue, care and conservation of animals.

Contact us at: societyofanimalrangers@gmail.com
